03 : About me

Motivation

In a typical day, I read a book by Inoue Tomohisa about the future of the economy and the potential collapse of employment in 2030. Recognizing the growing influence of AI in the coming years, I felt inspired to embark on a coding journey and pursue a career in the technology field..

Purpose

Through my past work experiences, I realized the limitations in my career growth. To overcome this, I have set a clear career path as a front-end developer, aiming to acquire in-demand skills that will allow me to continually challenge myself and shine in this rapidly evolving world.

Action

I started my coding journey in 2021 through the Zero to Mastery Academy. Learning has been a crucial process for me, exercising my brain and teaching me how to overcome errors by independently researching, reading technical documentation, and honing problem-solving skills before seeking assistance.

During my free time, I engage in HTML and JavaScript projects such as the NASA APOD project, reinforcing my coding abilities, debugging techniques, and troubleshooting skills. Additionally, I have gained proficiency in React and completed projects like Robofriend, SmartBrain, and Crwn-Clothing. I have an eagerness to continuously learn and adapt to new skills as needed.

Pursuit

With the knowledge and experience accumulated thus far, my goal is to provide profound and satisfying user experiences as a front-end developer for my future clients. I approach learning as an ongoing choice and integrate it into my daily practice, ensuring I stay at the forefront of technological advancements.
